## ✨ New

### Two-Factor Authentication Added ([#8](https://github.com/20sid02/shiplog-test/pull/8))

Now you can add an extra layer of security to your account with two-factor authentication. This means you'll need a special code from an authenticator app to log in, keeping your account even safer. You'll also get backup codes in case you lose access to the app.

### Custom Date Range Exports ([#4](https://github.com/20sid02/shiplog-test/pull/4))

Choose specific start and end dates for data exports. This feature helps you focus on the data that matters most. Export data with more precision and control.

### Dark Mode Now Available ([#1](https://github.com/20sid02/shiplog-test/pull/1))

Switch to a darker theme to reduce eye strain. This new mode is available in your account settings and syncs across devices. Your system preference is also respected for a seamless experience.
